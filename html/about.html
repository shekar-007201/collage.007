About Our College

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 1956

SR&BGNR GOVT.ARTS & SCIENCE was founded with a single vision — to create an institution where every student could grow intellectually, morally, and professionally. What started as a modest campus with 3 departments and 300 students has grown into one of Telangana's most respected colleges.

Over four decades, we have nurtured thousands of graduates who now lead in industries, academia, government, and society. Our commitment to quality education, research, and holistic development remains unchanged.
